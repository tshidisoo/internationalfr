Join us for a vibrant celebration of diversity at Guta Ra Mwari! Our Heritage Day, themed "God of All Nations," promises a day of fun, culture, and unity. We invite every Guta Ra Mwari member—Elders, Youths, and Little lens—to participate in this exciting competition and showcase their creativity.

Get ready to:

  • Design and wear stunning traditional attire from a country of your choice.
  • Cook and share mouthwatering traditional dishes from your chosen country.
  • Or even better, do both!

Let’s celebrate our heritage with joy and enthusiasm. The best part? All proceeds will go towards building the Centenary Museum and the Holy Shrine Canopy in Nyazura.
Don't miss out on this exciting event filled with cultural exchanges, delicious food, and beautiful attire!
